# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Chinese-speaking casual puzzle players who want a short daily thinking challenge on desktop or mobile.

## Product Purpose

FourFind presents sixteen words or images and asks players to discover four hidden categories. Success means the game is immediately understandable, pleasant to revisit, and fully usable across screen sizes.

## Brand Personality

Playful, thoughtful, tactile.

## Anti-references

Cold enterprise SaaS dashboards, rigid grayscale utilities, childish cartoon games, and decorative rainbow interfaces.

## Design Principles

- Open directly into the puzzle.
- Use color to explain categories and feedback.
- Keep every current action available on mobile.
- Make the game feel friendly without reducing clarity.
- Preserve a calm reading rhythm around a dense puzzle board.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, labels in addition to color, reduced-motion support, and touch targets suitable for mobile use.
