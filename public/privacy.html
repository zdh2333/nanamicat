Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-08

Nanami Cat ("we", "our", "the site") is a small daily word category puzzle game. This page explains what data we collect and how it is used. We try to keep things simple.

1. Analytics

The site may use a basic privacy-friendly analytics tool (such as Cloudflare Web Analytics) to understand overall traffic and improve the game. These tools record anonymous, aggregated information such as the approximate country, device type, and page visited. They are not used to identify you personally.

2. Cookies and Local Storage

The game uses your browser's localStorage to remember things like which puzzles you have completed, your best times, your nickname for the optional leaderboard, and your selected theme or language. Local storage stays on your device and is never transmitted to us as personally identifiable data.

A small number of cookies may be set by third-party services that load on the site (for example, an embedded video or an advertising partner, if and when such features are enabled).

3. Google AdSense

In the future we may display ads served by Google AdSense or a similar advertising partner. These partners may use cookies or similar technologies to deliver ads that are relevant to you, and to measure ad performance. You can learn more about how Google uses data from partner sites at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

4. Third-Party Advertising

When advertising is enabled, third-party vendors (including Google) may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this site or other sites. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Your Choices

You can clear all locally stored data at any time through your browser settings. Doing so will reset your puzzle progress, nickname, and theme preferences, and will not affect any leaderboard entries that may already be associated with your nickname on our servers.
