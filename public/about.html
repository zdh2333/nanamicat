About Nanami Cat

Nanami Cat is a free daily word category puzzle game. Every day a fresh puzzle appears: sixteen words on the board, and four hidden groups waiting to be found. Your job is to spot the connections.

Each puzzle has four color-coded groups, arranged from easy direct sets to one final "most abstract" group that ties everything together. It is designed to be solved in a few minutes — perfect for a coffee break, a commute, or a quiet evening.

Why it exists

We love the calm, tactile feeling of a good category puzzle and the small satisfaction of finishing one. Nanami Cat is our take on that feeling, with a friendly black cat mascot, a hand-drawn paper look, and a fresh challenge every day.

What you get

  • A new daily puzzle, every day.
  • A growing puzzle archive you can revisit any time.
  • Local progress tracking (no account required).
  • An optional leaderboard if you want to compare clear counts.
  • A community submission flow so anyone can suggest puzzle ideas.

Brand

Nanami is a small black cat who shows up to nudge you along — empty board, half-solved board, or full celebration. The visual style is paper, crayon, and doodle: warm, a little hand-made, and easy on the eyes.
