How to Play

Nanami Cat is a word category puzzle. Every day a fresh puzzle appears with sixteen words. Your job is to find four groups of four — each group shares a theme, and one group is the trickiest of the four.

Step 1: Read the board

The board shows sixteen words or short phrases. They are shuffled and may look unrelated at first — that is the point.

Step 2: Pick four

Tap or click on four items you think belong together. The selection is highlighted. You can deselect any item by clicking it again, or clear the whole selection with the Clear button.

Step 3: Submit

Once four items are selected, press Submit. If you have found a real group, those four items are locked in and a difficulty color is revealed. If not, you earn one mistake.

Step 4: Mistakes and giving up

You can make up to four wrong submissions per puzzle. After that you can still keep trying — you just cannot earn a perfect rating. If you are stuck, the Hint button will point you toward one of the remaining groups.

Step 5: Win and share

When you have found all four groups, the puzzle is complete. The trickiest group is highlighted as the most abstract one. You can share your time and mistake count, jump into the archive, or come back tomorrow for a new puzzle.

Example

A group might be breakfast staples: Youtiao · Bun · Soy milk · Shaobing. These four all share a clear theme, so they belong together.

Tips

  • Start with the obvious groups and work your way up.
  • If a word feels out of place, it is usually a "red herring" pointing to a near-miss.
  • Use Shuffle to break visual patterns that are misleading you.
  • Each puzzle can be revisited from the archive.
